My Projects

Enrollment Games

A website built for students to make their enrollment process easier. It included a timetable generator as well as, a teacher review system and a university resource system. Timetable generator was used to generate timetables without any clashes, according to students' needs. While the teacher review system made sure that students are provided with information that will help them select the teacher according to their needs. University resource system's sole purpose was to prepare the students and provide them with the learning material according to the course and the teacher. It also offered a functionality where students can test themselves by generating quizzes. This website gained 2600+ users and 15k+ views on its first year of launch!
